# Glossary

Evidence atom:
Minimal answer-supporting unit.

Background noise:
Text that appears related but does not support the answer.

Evidence-SNR:
Relevance normalized by local background statistics.

Deblending:
Separating overlapping claims or evidence sources.

Sparse reconstruction:
Choosing the smallest sufficient evidence set.

Context precision:
Fraction or ordering quality of relevant context.

Faithfulness:
Degree to which answer claims are supported by evidence.
