# RAG Prior Work

Relevant lines:
- Self-RAG: adaptive retrieval and critique with reflection tokens.
- Adaptive-RAG: choose retrieval strategy by query complexity.
- RAPTOR: recursive clustering and summaries for tree retrieval.
- GraphRAG: graph-structured retrieval for relational evidence.
- ColBERT: late interaction for fine-grained efficient retrieval.

Implication:
Our method must not merely be adaptive, hierarchical, or reranking.
It must show a distinct evidence-detection and sparse-reconstruction mechanism.
